# ThinkPod Overview

> An IDE for your thoughts, not your code. 100% Local.

## What is ThinkPod?

ThinkPod is a **fully local, AI-powered personal knowledge base** built as a desktop app. It is a note-taking app at its core — but the goal goes beyond that. The vision is to make you feel like there is a **smart, sentient being living inside your notes**: an entity that reads what you write, understands your thinking, asks the right questions, and helps you connect ideas you would have otherwise missed.

At its foundation, ThinkPod embeds an AI agent — **Wilfred** — deeply into every part of the document lifecycle: capturing, organizing, reviewing, and generating insight from your notes. Together, you and Wilfred become more than the sum of your parts — a unified system for unleashing your knowledge.

## The Core Differentiator

Most note-taking apps treat AI as a *reactive* tool — you ask, it answers. ThinkPod flips this: the agent is *proactive*, running continuously, building its own understanding of your vault over time. This is not just a feature difference. It's a philosophical one.

Imagine a brilliant research assistant who lives in your library, reads everything you've written, thinks about it while you sleep, and leaves you notes in the morning. No other consumer tool does this at this level. Obsidian, Notion, Bear — all passive. This is the gap ThinkPod fills.

## Key Features at a Glance

### 🔒 100% Local-First
- All notes stored as markdown files on your machine
- AI processing runs locally (or via your chosen provider)
- No cloud dependency, no subscription, no privacy trade-off
- Your data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly configure it

### 🤖 Agent-Native Design
- **Wilfred** is not a chatbot bolted on — he's integrated into your writing lifecycle
- Context-aware: automatically reads whatever document you open
- Proactive: sends you insights, research summaries, and suggestions
- Continuous: runs tasks in the background while you work

### ⚡ Fast Capture
- Quick note creation with keyboard shortcuts
- Voice dictation powered by local Whisper
- Drafts area for unfinished thoughts
- Minimal friction from idea to document

### 🎯 Smart Organization
- File tree with your **Vault** (notes folder)
- Categories: Journal, Ideas, Projects, People, Others
- Agent-assisted categorization and archiving
- Plain markdown files you own and control

## Who is ThinkPod For?

ThinkPod is ideal for:
- **Writers** who want an AI thinking partner, not just a spell checker
- **Researchers** who need to connect ideas across hundreds of notes
- **Privacy-conscious users** who want AI benefits without cloud dependency
- **Knowledge workers** who think in writing and need better tools
- **Anyone** who wants their notes to be more than just storage

## What Makes It Different?

| **Privacy** | 100% local option | Local files | Cloud-dependent |
| **Cost** | Free + optional AI costs | Free/Paid | Subscription required |
| **Agent Memory** | Cross-session, vault-wide | N/A | Limited context |
